Selected Projects

AI-Supported Workplace Learning | Microsoft Viva Capstone

Explored how AI could support adult learners in hybrid workplace environments by reducing fragmentation, surfacing contextual guidance, and supporting continuous learning.

Methods: Literature review, netnography, competitor analysis, SME interviews, focus groups, concept testing.

JUNO | Postpartum Care and ERAS Support

Designed a health support concept for postpartum care and recovery, focusing on care navigation, patient needs, and system-level adoption constraints.

Methods: Interviews, app review, journey mapping, service design, concept development.

Gyan AI | Clinical Learning Support

Designed an AI-supported learning platform for medical education, exploring how systems can support reasoning, retention, and productive friction in clinical learning.

Methods: User research, prototyping, evaluation, learning experience design.

Wearables + AI Behavior Insights

Investigated how wearable and AI-supported systems can help people interpret personal behavior, build self-awareness, and make sense of contextual health and lifestyle data.

Methods: Diary study, interviews, cognitive walkthrough, qualitative synthesis.
